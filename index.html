OUR STORY

Four Decades of Excellence

Legacy of Timeless Storytelling

Founded in 1976 by the visionary Mr. Manubhai Keshavlal Soni, From Virsad. Studio Ashoka has been at the forefront of photography excellence in Vadodara, Gujarat. What started as a passion for capturing moments has evolved into a legacy that spans over four decades.

Our commitment to storytelling through the lens has made us a household name for life's most precious celebrations. From intimate family portraits to grand destination weddings, we bring an artistic vision that transforms fleeting moments into timeless memories.

Located in the heart of Vadodara, our studio combines traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques, ensuring every photograph tells a story that will be cherished for generations.

OUR JOURNEY

Milestones Through Time

1976

The Beginning

Mr. Manubhai established Studio Ashoka with a vision to capture life's beautiful moments through the art of photography.

1982

Expansion Era

Expanded services to include wedding photography and introduced professional videography for the first time in Vadodara, establishing ourselves as the city’s premier choice for matrimonial celebrations.

2005

Digital Revolution

Embraced digital photography while maintaining our commitment to artistic excellence and storytelling, Started our Branch at Akota, Vadodara.

2020

Modern Legacy

Integrated contemporary techniques with traditional values, serving families across generations.
